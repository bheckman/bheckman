oregonmetro.gov

Description

A regional government site designed for the people.

Metro is an organization, lead by a council of elected officials, that works with communities and businesses to develop a plan for sustainable growth to the Portland metro area.

It manages parks, cemeteries, natural areas, and venues. It coordinates and funds regional transportation projects and conducts extensive research to inform land use development as the city of Portland grows

I worked as a user experience consultant during the discovery and planning phases of their website redesign. I met with internal stakeholders and facilitated collaborative discovery sessions with teams throughout the organization.

Following discovery, I developed a sitemap, wireframes, and technical requirements in the form of entity relationship diagrams.
