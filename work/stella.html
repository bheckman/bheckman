Stella McCartney's Universe

Description

An iPad application that encourages freeform exploration of Stella McCartney's whimsical world.

I oversaw the design and development of a custom iPad application for Stella McCartney. I worked closely with the Creative Director, Art Director, and our iOS development partner to conceptualize a user experience that would encourage users to freely explore a rich landscape of multimedia and interactive stories.

Employer: Wardenclyffe
Role: User experience design, project management
Graphic Design: Romain Corvez
Development: Caravan Interactive

A Mosiac of Stories

Users freely wander through the app by swiping left and right, using a pinching gesture to zoom in and out.

Focussed State

Double tapping on an individual tile launches the corresponding story full screen. Readers move between pages of a story by swiping left or right.
